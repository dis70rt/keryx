7mo • 

The Training and Placement Cell, Indian Institute of Technology (BHU) Varanasi is excited to announce the beginning of the Internship and Placement drive of session 2025-26 for recruiters.
The Institute ranks consistently in the Top IITs in the QS World University Rankings and stands as a testament to our commitment to excellence in education and research. With a robust alumni network and strong ties with various ministries of the Government of India, our students are well-equipped to lead and innovate in diverse fields such as Software Development, Finance, Consulting, Analytics, Information Technology, Cybersecurity, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Product Management and more.
The Indian Institute of Technology (Banaras Hindu University), Varanasi, draws the most astute talent in the nation through globally renowned examinations. Our halls have hosted thousands of industry leaders who now form an integral part of our esteemed alumni network, spanning the globe. Adding to our list of talented alumni this year will be 1800+ graduates from across 17 different programs, showcasing a vast diversity of talent.

Over 100+ years of excellence, IIT (BHU) has grown into a hub of innovation and impact with 15 departments & 3 interdisciplinary schools, 456 ongoing research projects, and 1439 consultancy projects in FY 2024-25. Our students have consistently delivered top global performances securing a Top-10 finish among 12k+ teams in IMC Prosperity Challenge 2025 , placed 2nd in Global IQC University Rankings in 2024 and 6th place in Shell Eco-Marathon 2025. We warmly invite all esteemed organizations to participate in our campus recruitment for the 2025-26 season.

The Training and Placement Cell, Indian Institute of Technology (BHU) Varanasi is proud to share the remarkable progress of this year’s Internship and Placement Drive. The institute consistently ranks among the top IITs in the QS World University Rankings, reinforcing its commitment to academic and research excellence.

This placement season has already witnessed 1209 Placement offers, including 263 Pre-Placement Offers (PPOs) and 450 internship offers. Our students continue to secure opportunities across diverse roles, including Software Development (SDE), Machine Learning & Quant, Analytics, Product & Business Management, and Core Engineering, with hiring still ongoing.

We are grateful to our prominent recruiters including Mastercard, Google, Databricks, Meesho, Piramal Group, Amazon, Wells Fargo, Uber, Rubrik, McKinsey & Company, Flipkart, Groww, Deutsche Bank, Samsung Electronics, NVIDIA and Microsoft, among many others, for their continued trust in our talent.

Indian Institute of Technology (Banaras Hindu University), Varanasi remains a hub of top-tier talent, ready to shape the future of technology, business, and innovation. We look forward to continued collaborations and even greater milestones ahead.

For more details, visit www.placement.iitbhu.ac.in or reach out at tpo@iitbhu.ac.in.
